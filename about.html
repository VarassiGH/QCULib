About QCULib

Empowering education through knowledge, innovation, and community

Welcome to QCU Library

Founded in 1998, the Quezon City University Library has been a cornerstone of academic excellence for over 25 years. Our library serves over 15,000 students and faculty members with comprehensive collections, cutting-edge technology, and innovative services designed to enhance the learning experience.

As the heart of academic life at QCU, we strive to create an environment that inspires intellectual curiosity, supports research endeavors, and fosters a community of lifelong learners.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality resources and services that empower students and faculty to achieve academic excellence through innovative learning spaces, diverse collections, and expert guidance.

Our Vision

To be a leading academic library that fosters innovation, collaboration, and lifelong learning, serving as a technological and intellectual hub that adapts to the evolving needs of our diverse community.

Our Core Values

Accessibility

Ensuring equal access to resources and services for all members of our community

Innovation

Embracing new technologies and approaches to enhance the learning experience

Collaboration

Working together with faculty, students, and other institutions to achieve common goals

Excellence

Striving for the highest quality in our collections, services, and staff expertise

Our History

1998

QCU Library was established with an initial collection of 5,000 books

2005

Major expansion of the library facilities, doubling the available space

2012

Launch of comprehensive digital resources and online catalog system

2018

Renovation of learning spaces and introduction of collaborative zones

2023

Implementation of AI-powered research assistance and expanded digital collections
