How to Access Our Services

Follow these simple steps to make the most of our library resources

1

Register for Library Access

Visit the front desk with your student ID to activate your library account. This one-time registration enables access to all our services.

2

Browse & Select Resources

Explore our physical collection in the library or search our digital catalog online to find the resources you need.

3

Reserve or Borrow

Use our online reservation system or visit the service desk to borrow books, equipment, or reserve study spaces.

4

Enjoy & Return

Make use of our resources and return them by the due date to avoid late fees. Online renewals are available for most items.
