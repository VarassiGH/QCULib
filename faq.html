Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our library services and policies.

How do I register for library access?

Visit the front desk with your student ID to activate your library account. Registration is free for all students and faculty.

Required documents:

  • Valid student/faculty ID
  • Proof of current enrollment/employment
  • Contact information

What is the loan period for borrowed books?

The standard loan period is 14 days. You can renew your books online if no other user has reserved them.

Different loan periods apply for different materials:

  • General books: 14 days
  • Reserved books: 3 days
  • Reference materials: In-library use only
  • Multimedia: 7 days

Are there late fees for overdue items?

Yes, a small fee is charged for overdue items. Please return your items on time to avoid penalties.

Fee structure:

  • General books: ₱10 per day
  • Reserved books: ₱20 per day
  • Multimedia: ₱15 per day

Maximum overdue fee is the replacement cost of the item.

Can I reserve study rooms online?

Yes, you can reserve study rooms through our online reservation system. Visit the Study Rooms section for more details.

Study room guidelines:

  • Reservations can be made up to 2 weeks in advance
  • Maximum reservation time: 3 hours per day
  • Groups of 2 or more have priority for group study rooms
  • No-shows after 15 minutes forfeit the reservation

How do I access digital resources remotely?

You can access our digital resources remotely using your library credentials. Simply visit our Digital Library portal and log in with your student/faculty ID and password.

Available digital resources include:

  • E-books and e-journals
  • Academic databases
  • Research papers and archives
  • Multimedia learning materials

Can alumni access library resources?

Yes, alumni can register for a special Alumni Library Membership. Visit the Alumni Relations Office or the library front desk for more information on fees and access privileges.

Alumni members have access to:

  • Borrowing privileges (limited collection)
  • In-library computer access
  • Selected digital resources
  • Special alumni events and workshops

Can't find what you're looking for?

Our library staff is here to help. Contact us directly or submit your question below.
